Beef Tacos

Description:Flavorful ground beef seasoned with taco spices, served in crispy taco shells with your favorite toppings

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Taco shells or tortillas

Instructions:

  1. In a skillet, cook ground beef with onion and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  2. Stir in taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5 minutes until thickened.
  3. Spoon beef mixture into taco shells or tortillas.
  4. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream if desired.
