Chicken Stir-Fry

Description:A quick and flavorful stir-fry with tender chicken and crisp vegetables, coated in a savory-sweet sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced thinly
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil and sesame oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Add garlic and ginger, cooking until fragrant.
  2. Add chicken slices and cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. TStir in soy sauce and hoisin sauce. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve over rice or noodles.
