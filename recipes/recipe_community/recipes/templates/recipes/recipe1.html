Spaghetti Carbonara

Description:Classic Italian pasta dish with a creamy, cheesy sauce made from eggs and Parmesan, combined with crispy pancetta.

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and reserve some pasta water.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and cook pancetta until crispy. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs and Parmesan together.
  4. Toss the spaghetti in the pan with pancetta and garlic. Remove from heat and quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding a bit of reserved pasta water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper. Serve immediately.
